Blue Serene

Turning off the highway that extends through Kerala’s coastline, guests will travel along a narrow road that winds past hamlets fringed by stretches of paddy and coconut groves, to arrive at Blue Serene.

Blue Serene is contained in a single block designed in contemporary architecture, surrounded by exquisitely landscaped grounds and set beside the Enamavu Lake, a pristine stretch of Kerala’s northern backwaters The palm fringed beaches and backwaters in the north of Kerala are stunningly beautiful and most importantly, for most part, far removed from the tourist trail and therefore tranquil. Northern Kerala is also an area with its own distinctively vibrant folk culture.
