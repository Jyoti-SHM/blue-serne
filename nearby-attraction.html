Places to Visit Nearby

Cheraman Juma Masjid

Cheraman Juma Masjid, located in Kodungallur, Kerala, is one of India's oldest mosques, built around 629 AD. Established by Malik Deenar and named after the Chera King Cheraman Perumal, it symbolizes the cultural fusion between Arab traders and the Malabar community. The mosque uniquely blends Kerala architectural elements with Islamic influences, making it a revered site for worshippers and tourists. It has long been a center of religious and cultural harmony.

Muziris Heritage Project

The Muziris Heritage Project, initiated by the Government of Kerala, aims to preserve and showcase the ancient port city of Muziris in Ernakulam and Thrissur districts. Once a key trade hub between the East and West (1st century BC to 4th century AD), the project revives its legacy through archaeological excavations, conservation, and the development of museums and heritage trails. It offers visitors a comprehensive experience of the region's rich history and multicultural heritage.

Chavakkad Beach

Chavakkad Beach, near Guruvayur in Kerala, is a serene coastal destination known for its golden sands, coconut palms, and clear blue waters. The confluence of the Arabian Sea and a nearby river adds to its charm. It's ideal for relaxation, beachside activities, and witnessing stunning sunsets, offering a tranquil coastal ambiance.

Thrissur Town

Thrissur Town, known as Kerala's cultural capital, is celebrated for its rich heritage, historical sites, and vibrant festivals. Famous for the grand Thrissur Pooram festival, the city is home to landmarks like the Vadakkunnathan Temple and Shakthan Thampuran Palace. Blending tradition and modernity, Thrissur offers a unique and enriching experience for visitors.

Snehatheeram Beach

Snehatheeram Beach, located near Thrissur in Kerala, is a tranquil and scenic spot known for its clean sands and gentle waves. With its serene environment, lush greenery, and well-maintained facilities, it’s a great place for relaxation and family outings. The beach also features a picturesque setting for enjoying sunsets and leisurely strolls along the shoreline.

Punnathur Kota Elephant sanctuary

Punnathur Kota Elephant Sanctuary, located near Guruvayur in Kerala, is a renowned refuge for elephants, primarily housing those associated with the nearby Guruvayur Temple. Spread across a lush, expansive area, the sanctuary provides a natural habitat for these majestic animals.

Palayur Church

Palayur Church, located in Palayur near Thrissur, Kerala, is one of the oldest Christian churches in India, believed to have been established in 52 AD by St. Thomas the Apostle. The church is renowned for its historical significance, ancient architecture, and spiritual heritage.

Guruvayoor Temple

Guruvayoor Temple in Kerala is a highly revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Krishna. Known for its ancient traditions and intricate architecture, it attracts numerous devotees and is famous for its vibrant festivals, including the Guruvayoor Pooram.

Sakthan Thampuran Palace, Museum & Zoo

Sakthan Thampuran Palace, located in Thrissur, Kerala, is a historic royal residence now functioning as a museum. It showcases artifacts, antiques, and royal memorabilia, reflecting the grandeur of the erstwhile Kochi dynasty. The adjacent Thrissur Zoo, established in 1885, is one of the oldest in India and features a variety of wildlife, including mammals, birds, and reptiles. Together, the palace and zoo offer visitors a blend of cultural heritage and natural history.

Athirappily Water Falls

Athirappilly Waterfalls, located in the Thrissur district of Kerala, is a stunning natural attraction renowned for its majestic cascade and lush surroundings. Often referred to as the "Niagara of India," the falls plunge dramatically from a height of about 80 feet, creating a breathtaking view.

Cochin International Air Port

Cochin International Airport, located in Nedumbassery near Kochi, Kerala, is a major international gateway serving the region. Renowned for being the world's first fully solar-powered airport, it offers modern amenities, efficient services, and connections to numerous domestic and international destinations. The airport's eco-friendly initiatives and state-of-the-art facilities make it a key hub for travelers and a symbol of sustainable development in aviation.
